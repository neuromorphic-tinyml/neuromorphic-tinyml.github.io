About

Welcome to the Neuromorphic tinyML Workshop 2022. This two-day workshop is organized under the MoU of the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) and Washington University in St. Louis, sponsored by Strategic Partners Acquisition Readiness Contract (SPARC).

  • Workshop On Neuromorphic Systems & tinyML

    Here, we provide the hands-on opportunity to learn and practice state-of-the-art implementations related to tinyML and neuromorphic systems. We invite researchers, faculty members, industry professionals, and students to participate and provide their feedback.

  • Neuromorphic System

    These systems mimic neuro-biological architectures present in the nervous system using physical artificial neurons (made from silicon) to do computations. It is the intersection of Neurophysiology, Computer Science and Electrical Engineering.

  • tinyML

    Big is not always better

    tinyML is a field of study in Machine Learning and Embedded Systems that explores the types of models which can run on small, low-powered devices like microcontrollers. It enables low-latency, and low power model inference at edge devices.
